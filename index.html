Cookies & privacy policy

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files which websites can use to store some pieces of information. They are stored on your browser when the website is accessed. Cookies help a site function better or provide additional functionalities and experience

How are cookies used here?

There are essential cookies designed only to track whether you have defined your cookie preference before, so you do not have to keep dealing with that privacy policy banner again every time you visit.

Asides from that, there is one 3rd party cookie, which is for the usage of Google Analytics, which is the standard tool website managers use to understand how a website is used and where their visitors come from, so that the site and the user experience can be further improved. For more information on how Google handles the data, please refer to their policy.
